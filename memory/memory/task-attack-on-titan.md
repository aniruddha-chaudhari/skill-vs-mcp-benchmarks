# Attack on Titan Summary

**Attack on Titan** (Japanese: 進撃の巨人, _Shingeki no Kyojin_) is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Hajime Isayama.

## Overview

The story is set in a dystopian world where humanity lives within three enormous walls to protect themselves from gigantic man-eating humanoids called **Titans**. For over a century, humanity has lived in fear behind these walls.

## Main Characters

- **Eren Yeager** - The protagonist who vows to exterminate all Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother
- **Mikasa Ackerman** - Eren's adoptive sister and skilled warrior
- **Armin Arlert** - Eren's best friend, known for his intelligence

## Story

The series begins with the Titans breaching the outer wall, leading to the destruction of Eren's hometown and his mother's death. This tragedy drives Eren to join the military and fight against the Titans.

As the story progresses, dark secrets about the world are revealed, the Titans' origins are uncovered, and the narrative expands into a complex exploration of power, freedom, cycle of hatred, and humanity's struggle for survival.

## Publication

- **Manga**: Serialized in Kodansha's Bessatsu Shōnen Magazine from September 2009 to April 2021, collected in 34 volumes
- **Anime**: Produced by Wit Studio (seasons 1-3) and MAPPA (season 4), with the final season ending in 2021

## Themes

- Survival and humanity's will to live
- Power and its corrupting influence
- Freedom vs. oppression
- Cycles of violence and revenge
- Identity and memory

The series has become a global phenomenon, known for its intense action, complex plot twists, and mature themes exploring the darker aspects of human nature.
